# 📊 Customer Churn & Revenue Analysis

## 🔍 Problem Statement
Analyze customer behavior and revenue trends to identify churn and provide business insights.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- SQL
- Power BI

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- Total Revenue, Orders, Churn Rate
- Revenue Trend
- Revenue by City
- Customer Type Analysis
- Top Customers

---

## 🧠 Key Insights
- Revenue increased by ~30% from March to May
- 47% churn rate observed
- Majority customers are one-time buyers
- Bangalore is highest revenue contributor

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Introduce loyalty programs
- Offer discounts to churned customers
- Focus on underperforming cities
